**Body:** Influenza season is approaching. The flu vaccine is your best defense against getting sick and spreading the virus to others. It's quick, safe, and recommended for everyone 6 months and older. Visit your local clinic or pharmacy to get protected. #FluShot #HealthyCommunity #Prevention

**Suggested image:** A person receiving a vaccine, smiling.

### Post 2 (educational)
**Headline:** How Does Flu Spread?
**Body:** Flu viruses spread through droplets when people cough, sneeze, or talk. You can also get flu by touching contaminated surfaces. Simple steps like washing hands, covering coughs, and staying home when sick can help protect everyone. #FluFacts #HealthyHabits

**Suggested image:** Illustration of a person covering cough with elbow.
